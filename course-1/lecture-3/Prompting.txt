* Prompt is the input to the AI
* 6 core elements of good prompting
    * Role - tell AI what role to assume (like imagine you are a travel advisor)
    * Context - give some context of what are you problems or what you want to achieve
    * Instructions - it is the clear goal
    * Formatting - tell how you want the response to be
    * Boundaries - tell boundaries like budget, location of hotel etc
    * Example - give some example
* You can go one step beyond and ask AI to generate the prompt for you. It is called Meta-Prompting.
* A good AI Prompter is really good at tweaking Prompts. Refine your prompts so that you reach the optimum level.
* Tweaking of the prompt is done in iterations, by carefully reviewing the information/response given by AI
* Prompt Priming
    * It means preparing a thread with as much relevant context as possible, before you send the original prompt
